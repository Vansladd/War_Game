##TP_PARAMS##
Instructions:
1. To make a ball selection area, click and hold with your mouse button where you would like the top left of the rectangle to be. While holding the mouse button down, move your mouse down/right to expand the rectangle.
2. When you are happy with the size and shape of the rectangle, release the left mouse button.
3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 for each of the four pictures. If you are unhappy with any of the rectangles, repeat steps 1 and 2 to generate a new rectangle.
NOTE:  If the rectangle you create is not big enough for the 12 balls to be placed, the rectangle will be resized to accomodate all 12 balls.
