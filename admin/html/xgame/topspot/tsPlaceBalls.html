Ball Placements for Next Competition

##TP_PARAMS##

Instructions:

1. Choose a picture by clicking the small pictures on the left side of the panel above.

2. Place the 12 balls on the picture displayed in the main window by clicking with the mouse button.

3. Once you have placed all 12 balls onto the surface of the picture you can select any of the balls you have placed (move you mouse cursor over the desired ball and when it changes colour from red to yellow, click the mouse and it will change colour to green). Once the ball you wish to move is selected (green) you can alter its position by dragging it with the mouse or adjust its position with the arrow keys.

4. Complete steps 1, 2 and 3 for each of the 4 pictures.

5. click the ENTER button on the bottom right of the main panel and confirm your selections. The details of the competition you have just created will be saved.
